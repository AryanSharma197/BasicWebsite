HOSTEL

ABVGIET provides residential facilities for girls within the campus only.
It is surrounded by a lush green garden frequently maintained by Gardner's nourishing a peaceful mindset.
There are two girls hostel in the campus:
Hostel Name Capacity No. of rooms Sharing Ratio
Parvati Girls Hostel 40 bed 10 4 girls per room
Saraswati Girls Hostel 99 beds 33 3 girls per room
The hostel is secured with Hi-Tech cameras and home guards. There is a special room for Wardens that facilitates security monitoring.
The hostel community organizes various cultural activities often within the hostel only.

Note: Boys hostel is under construction.
