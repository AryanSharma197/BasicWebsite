SPORTS 🏆

Sport at ABVGIET teaches students about sportsmanship, teamwork, and courage.
Students learn to be gracious winners and good losers. Sports play an important part in the life of most students, with a 90 percent participation rate.
Outdoor Sports facilities like Cricket, Football, Volleyball, Basketball, Athletics are available at the ABVGIET campus itself under the supervision of full-time sports coaches.
Indoor recreational games like table tennis, carom, chess tables are available in the common rooms.

List of available sports → 🏅

Volleyball 🏐
Volleyball is a popular team sport played on a rectangular court divided by a net, with teams of six players each aiming to score points by sending the ball over the net and into the opponent's court.

Basketball 🏀
Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played between two teams of five players each, where points are scored by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop.

Football ⚽
Football, also known as soccer in some regions, is a globally beloved team sport played between two teams of eleven players each, aiming to score goals by getting a ball into the opposing team's net.

Cricket 🏏
Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams, with the objective of scoring runs by hitting a ball and defending wickets in a match that combines elements of strategy, skill, and endurance.

Badminton 🏸
Badminton is a racquet sport played indoors or outdoors by either two players (singles) or four players (doubles), with the objective of scoring points by hitting a shuttlecock over the net.

Chess ♔
Chess is a classic board game of strategic skill and foresight, played between two opponents who maneuver diverse pieces on an 8x8 board to checkmate the opponent's king.

Table Tennis 🏓
Table tennis, also known as ping pong, is a fast-paced indoor sport played with paddles and a lightweight ball across a rectangular table divided by a net, involving quick reflexes and precise shots.
